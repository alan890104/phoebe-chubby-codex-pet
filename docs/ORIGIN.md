# Where did Phoebe Chubby come from?

Short answer: from years of reposting, editing, and fan remixes.

Phoebe is a friendly and devout priest from the Order of the Deep in Wuthering Waves. The community gradually transformed her into the round, extremely expressive meme creature commonly called 菲比啾比 / Phoebe Chubby.

The exact authorship chain of every viral image is not reliably recoverable. Some community write-ups describe an early Korean-community visual lineage and later waves of edits, but that is not enough to name one person as the verified author of all versions. This repository therefore does not make that claim.

The images used by this Codex pet were generated with GPT Image 2 and independently assembled into a sprite atlas. This is an unofficial, non-commercial fan project.

If you have a reliable primary source that improves this history, please open an issue and include the source.
