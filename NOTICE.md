# Project notice

This is an unofficial, non-commercial fan project.

The pet images in this repository were generated with GPT Image 2, then assembled into a Codex pet animation set. They are not a direct repack of a third-party sticker collection.

The exact first creator of every image and variation in the circulating Phoebe Chubby meme family is difficult to verify after many reposts, edits, and remixes. If you know a reliable primary source or are a relevant creator, please open an attribution issue. We will happily improve the record.

The code, docs, and GPT Image 2-generated pet files we created for this repository are shared under MIT. Phoebe, Wuthering Waves, and existing third-party names, characters, and rights remain with their respective owners.

There are no ads or paid downloads.
